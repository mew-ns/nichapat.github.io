Global Model

• In-app payment integration — Hommi was an early-stage home services marketplace. We conducted the research for market insights to decide on in-app payment strategies.

• Global benchmarking — By analyzing transactions flows of 3 global marketplaces, I found a standard model used at scale. They all leveraged escrow system as a trust mechanism.

Local Competitor

• Competitive mapping — To identify market opportunities, I mapped 5 local competitors not only an escrow but across dimensions, then simplifed complex and fragmented data into a single standardized overview.

Key Insights

The research revealed that 2 out of 5 competitors utilized escrow payment. Meanwhile, platform fees reached as high as 47%, starting from 10%.

Payment Strategy

• Financial trust & affordability — Hommi decided to bridge local gaps by implementing an escrow model and offering a competitive fee, from 5% up to only 10%.

• Milestone-based payments — We also introduced a unique staged installment to manage cash flow and to prevent high-ticket project dropouts.
